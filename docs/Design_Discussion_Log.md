# Design Discussion Log

This document tracks the discussion points, open questions, and decisions made while producing the requirement clarification, architecture review, and detail design for the Unified Service Scheduler (Appointment Scheduler) feature. It is intended to give a reviewer visibility into how AI assistance was used during the design process: what was raised, by whom, what was decided, and why.

**How to read the "Raised By" / "Decided By" columns:** "AI" means the point was surfaced unprompted as part of an analysis or review the candidate requested (e.g. "review this file," "list ambiguities"). "Candidate" means the candidate identified the issue or question themselves. "Decided By" is almost always the candidate — the AI's role was to lay out interpretations, tradeoffs, and options; the candidate made the actual calls. Where the AI's suggestion was accepted as-is, that's noted explicitly.
